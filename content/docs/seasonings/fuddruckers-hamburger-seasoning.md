# Fuddruckers Hamburger Seasoning

## Ingredients

- 1 tsp. paprika
- 3/4 tsp. ground black pepper
- 1/2 + 1/8 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. brown sugar
- 1/8 tsp. garlic powder
- 1/8 tsp. onion powder
- 1/8 tsp. ground cayenne pepper

## Instructions

- Combine all ingredients in bowl
